# Euler's 1st Substitution

$$ \int \dfrac{\mathrm{d} x}{\sqrt{x^2 + c}} $$

given $c = \pm 1$

$$ \int \dfrac{\mathrm{d} x}{\sqrt{x^2 + c}} = \mathrm{arcsinh}(x) + C $$

$$ \int \dfrac{\mathrm{d} x}{\sqrt{x^2 + c}} = \mathrm{arccosh}(x) + C $$
